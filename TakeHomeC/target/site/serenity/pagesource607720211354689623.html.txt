<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=phone&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Noun">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">phone</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/phones#English" title="phones">phones</a></b>)

<ol><li>A <a href="/wiki/device" title="device">device</a> for <a href="/wiki/transmit" title="transmit">transmitting</a> <a href="/wiki/conversation" title="conversation">conversations</a> and other sounds in <a href="/wiki/real_time" title="real time">real time</a> across <a href="/wiki/distance" title="distance">distances</a>, now often a small portable unit also capable of running <a href="/wiki/software" title="software">software</a> etc.

<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">My <b>phone</b> ran out of battery.</i></div></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">There's an old-fashioned <b>phone</b> in the doctor's waiting room - it doesn't work anymore, of course.</i></div></dd></dl></li></ol>
